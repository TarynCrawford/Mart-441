Who is Taryn?

Born in January, Taryn is a proud Media Arts major with a love for games, animation, and digital art. She is expected to graduate in Spring 2023 and has been on the deans list for most of her time in college.

She is inspired by her family, japanese animation and aesthetics, and her furry best friend Lily. She is also happily married and beginning her adult life by building her future home by hand with her husband.
